Most Wanted

Anarkali

An Anarkali dress is a traditional Indian outfit that takes its inspiration from the attire worn during the Mughal era, particularly during the reign of Emperor Akbar. The dress is named after the legendary figure Anarkali, whom we discussed earlier. The Anarkali dress is known for its elegant and flowing design, characterized by a fitted bodice and a flared skirt.

Gown

A gown is a formal and elegant type of dress that is typically worn on special occasions, such as formal events, galas, proms, weddings, and other upscale gatherings. Gowns are characterized by their sophisticated design, luxurious materials, and often elaborate embellishments

Georgette flare top

A flare top, also known as a flared top or a peplum top, is a type of women's clothing that features a flared or ruffled extension from the waist down, creating a distinctive silhouette. Flare tops are known for their flattering and feminine design, as well as their ability to add volume and movement to an outfit
